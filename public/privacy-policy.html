Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

The Everyday Interiorist ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website, submit a contact request, or engage our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, and project location.
  • Project details: design preferences, budget range, property details, and files you share.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device data, and usage analytics.
  • Communications: messages submitted through forms, email, or consultation requests.

2. How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide design consultations.
  • Prepare proposals, project scopes, and service agreements.
  • Operate, secure, and improve our website and services.
  • Send service-related updates and, where permitted, marketing communications.
  • Comply with legal, accounting, and regulatory obligations.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable, we process personal data based on:

  • Your consent.
  • Performance of a contract or pre-contract request.
  • Our legitimate interests in operating and improving our business.
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tools to remember preferences, measure site performance, and understand visitor behavior. You can manage cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers supporting hosting, analytics, communications, and operations.
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting, insurance) where necessary.
  • Government authorities or regulators when required by law.
  • Business transferees in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, including project records, legal compliance, and dispute resolution obligations.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Request portability of eligible personal information.

To exercise rights, contact us using the details below. We may request identity verification before processing requests.

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices and encourage reviewing their policies separately.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes become effective when posted on this page with the revised effective date.
